# Syllabus Review

**Assignment:** AI-supported critical read of the V0.7 syllabus prototype (~60-90 minutes)

The course provided a medium-fidelity prototype syllabus and asked students to use an AI system to explore it — learning, forming opinions, and examining the workload. The deliverable is a high-level summary of how AI was leveraged and three takeaways for iterating the syllabus.

## Approach

Used Claude Opus to dialogue through the syllabus section by section, cross-referencing against ongoing projects and prior coursework to identify where the five-capability framework connects to real work.
