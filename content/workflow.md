# Workflow

## How Notes Get Made

I take notes by chatting with Claude Opus during and after class. The conversation is freeform — I talk through ideas, reactions, and questions in real time. When a conversation has enough substance, I have Claude summarize it into a structured page and add it to this site.

## How the Site Gets Updated

General edits — adding new pages, updating the index, reorganizing sections — are all done through Claude Code. I describe what I want and Claude writes the markdown, commits it, and pushes to GitHub. Quartz builds the site from there.

The whole pipeline is conversational. There's no manual file editing or CMS. Everything flows through chat.
